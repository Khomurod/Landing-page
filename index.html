Frequently Asked Questions

Is SafeHaul compliant with FMCSA/DOT DQ requirements? +
Yes, our DQ module is built specifically to track and maintain compliance with FMCSA and DOT regulations, complete with audit logs and auto-reminders.
Can I export all my data at any time? +
Absolutely. You own your data. Our platform allows complete data export to remain GDPR and Clean compliance ready.
Are e-signatures legally binding? +
Yes. SafeHaul’s e-doc module produces tamper-proof, digitally sealed documents with completion certificates that meet E-SIGN Act requirements.
How does bulk SMS compliance work? Are there additional charges? +
Our SMS gateway handles opt-out management and sender registration. While unlimited sends are included in the $299 plan, standard carrier pass-through fees (fraction of a cent per message) may apply.
What support is available for onboarding? +
We offer guided onboarding tours directly in the app, plus comprehensive email and priority support to ensure your team is set up for success immediately.
Is there a contract or setup fee? +
No. We believe in transparent, flat monthly pricing with no hidden setup fees and no long-term lock-in contracts.
